Pod Structure & Roles

A clear line of succession prevents confusion and ensures everyone understands their role. Roles are rotated periodically to spread knowledge and prevent burnout.

Activating a Backup: The Hand-Off Process

Clear, simple procedures for both planned and unplanned absences are key to a seamless transition. Follow these steps to activate the pod.

üóìÔ∏è Planned Absence (PTO)

1. Inform Pod

Send a Slack message to the pod channel with OOO dates and link to the Success Plan.

2. Brief (Optional)

Schedule an optional call with the pod to discuss account details if needed.

3. Update Systems

  • Update your calendar
  • Set Gmail OOO (include pod group email)
  • Set Slack OOO (name primary backups)

ü§í Unplanned Absence (Sick)

1. Manager Communicates

Manager informs the Pod Leader of the unexpected absence.

2. Pod Leader Delegates

The Pod Leader delegates urgent tasks like ticket reviews and customer calls.

3. Backup Informs Client

The Primary Backup communicates the absence to the account's named contacts.
